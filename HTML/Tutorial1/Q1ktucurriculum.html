Syllabus

Semester 7

Artificial Intelligence

Introduction to AI, intelligent agents, search algorithms, knowledge representation, expert systems, and machine learning basics.

Compiler Design Lab

Lexical analysis, parser implementation, syntax-directed translation, code generation, and compiler tools.

Elective I

Students can choose a subject from the list of department electives.

Project Phase I

Project identification, literature survey, design, planning, and initial implementation.

Seminar

Presentation and discussion on a recent technology or research paper.

Elective II

Students can choose another department elective based on their interest.

Industrial Safety Engineering

Industrial hazards, safety management, accident prevention, and safety regulations.

Semester 8

Distributed Computing

Distributed systems, communication models, synchronization, distributed algorithms, and cloud computing concepts.

Project Phase II

Project implementation, testing, documentation, and final demonstration.

Comprehensive Viva

Oral examination covering the complete B.Tech curriculum.

Elective III

Advanced elective chosen by the student.

Advanced elective chosen by the student.
